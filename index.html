What is Slaask?

Slaask supercharges your Slack to function as a real-time customer service app shared by your team.
Through the Slaask livechat on your site, communication from your visitors/leads/customers funnels directly into your Slack.

Using Slaask, am I able to provide support to my leads and customers via a 'bot'?

For sure! With Slaask, you can create a Smart Sales Assistant for your company, otherwise known as a "bot."
